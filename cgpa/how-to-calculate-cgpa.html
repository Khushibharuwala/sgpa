CGPA Kaise Calculate Kare – Step by Step

Step 1: Har Subject ka Grade Point aur Credit Note Karo

Universities har subject ko credits dete hain (jaise 3, 4 ya 5 credits). Har subject mein aapko ek grade milta hai jo grade point mein convert hota hai (jaise A+ = 9, A = 8 etc.).

Step 3: Sab Points ko Add Karo (Total Grade Points)

36 + 32 + 21 = 89

Step 4: Total Credits se Divide Karo

CGPA = Total Points ÷ Total Credits = 89 ÷ 11 = 8.09

SGPA vs CGPA

SGPA = Sirf ek semester ka average
CGPA = Saare semesters ka combined average

CGPA to Percentage Conversion (University-wise)

  • CBSE / Most Central Universities: Percentage = CGPA × 9.5
  • VTU (2015-18 Scheme): Percentage = (CGPA – 0.75) × 10
  • Anna University: Percentage = CGPA × 10
  • Mumbai University: Percentage = (CGPA × 7.1) + 11

CGPA calculate karne ka yeh tarika sabse accurate hai kyunki yeh har subject ke credits ko importance deta hai. Agar aapka koi subject 5 credits ka hai aur dusra 2 credits ka, toh 5 credit wale subject ka zyada weightage hoga.
